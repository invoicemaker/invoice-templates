## About

These templates are designed for freelancers, small business owners, contractors, and
consultants who need clean, ready-to-use invoice documents without the hassle of building
one from scratch. Every template is fully customizable — just open, fill in your details,
and send.

---

## How to Use

1. Browse the template list below
2. Download the PDF or Excel (.xlsx) version
3. Open in Adobe Acrobat, Microsoft Excel, or Google Sheets/Docs
4. Fill in your business name, client details, line items, and payment terms
5. Save and send to your client

For a faster workflow, use the free online invoice generator at
[invoicemaker.com](https://invoicemaker.com/templates) to create, send, and track
invoices in minutes.
